Introduction to Math!

Welcome to our math segment! Here we are going to learn about using math ideas in real life. Math can be useful outside the classroom, and you will likely encounter in your everday life. Below we will quiz you on your ability to use your math skills in the real world. But first, we will be reviewing some key concepts.

Basic counting skills are very important! Be sure you are familiar with your basic numberline before continuing!

In your day to day life you will encounter addition in many places! To the right is an addition chart. If you choose a number on the top and a number on the left, the table tells you the total when those numbers are added together. Just see where the top number's column, and left side number's row intersect, and there is the answer!

You may not think fractions would ever show up in life outside math class, but you'd be mistaken! Fractions help us understand how to divide up a whole thing easily and fairly. Make sure you are familiar with how to write and read fractions.
