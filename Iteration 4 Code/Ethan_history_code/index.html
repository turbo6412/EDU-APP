Asia

-China's longest dynasty was the Zhou Dynasty, lasting 1122-255 BC, about 867 years!

-Two of the most popular beliefs, Hinduism and Buddhism, originated in India!

-Between 1639 to 1853, Japan closed themselves off from the world, about 214 years!
North America

-America gained their independence on July 4th, 1776!

-Mexico is home to Chichen Itza, one of the seven wonders of the modern world!

-English and French are the two official languages of Canada!
South America

-Brazil holds one of the seven wonders of the modern wold, Christ the Redeemer!

-The ancient city, Machu Picchu, is found in Peru, another one of the seven wonders of the modern world!

-There are four South American countries that don't consider Spanish as their first language: Brazil, Guyana, Suriname, and French Guiana!
Africa

-Egypt is home to one of the seven wonders of the ancient world, the Great Pyramids of Giza!

-Africa is home to the Nile River is the longest river in the world!

-Africa has more than 2000 languages!
Europe

-Queen Elizabeth II began her rule in 1952

-France is known for having the Eiffel Tower in January 28, 1887!

-It is believed Europe is named after the Greek mythology princess, Europa!
Australia

-Australia was first discovered by the Dutch in 1606

-Australia holds the oldest continental crust on Earth!

-Australia is also home to a lot of exotic animals and species!
